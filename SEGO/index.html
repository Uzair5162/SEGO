Who We Are

We are a student-led initiative to help direct engineering-bound high school students like us into relevent opportunities to expand their interests. We organize trips to engineering workplaces and work with universities to empower youth to pursue careers in engineering. We attend Glenforest Secondary School with a passion for technology, creativity and design and we hope to explore the various careers in fields of interest, as well as gain exposure to workplace responsibilities.

Our Aim

We wish to explore the professional side of the engineering field, the day-to-day responsibilities of an engineer in the workplace, how new technological innovations impact the jobs of the workers, as well as information on job security and the demand for various types of jobs in the field.

Our Goals

  • Facilitate workshops that educate members on the specific fields in engineering, which include job prospects as well as workplace conditions
  • Organized tours to well-established and respectable companies. This gives students a first-hand experience on what the job of an engineer is actually like
  • Info-sessions from workers in the field so that they may share their experiences with prospective engineers
