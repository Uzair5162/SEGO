Meet the Team

A student-led initiative directing engineering-bound high schoolers into relevant opportunities, building experience.

SEGO is Ontarioâ€™s own club for passionate and motivated students who wish to learn more about the various fields in the engineering line of work. We are a student-led initiative to help direct engineering-bound high school students like us into relevant opportunities to expand their interests. We organize trips to engineering workplaces and work with universities to empower youth to pursue careers in engineering. We attend Glenforest Secondary School with a passion for technology, creativity and design and we hope to explore the various careers in fields of interest, as well as gain exposure to workplace responsibilities.

Our Goals

  • Facilitate workshops that educate members on the specific fields in engineering, which include job prospects as well as workplace conditions
  • Organized tours to well-established and respectable companies. This gives students a first-hand experience on what the job of an engineer is actually like
  • Info-sessions from workers in the field so that they may share their experiences with prospective engineers
